Welcome to the Newcastle Coders Group

1st Wednesday of each month in the ICT building at the University of Newcastle

  • View the latest meeting announcement on meetup:
  • Have you joined our facebook group?

Learn

We are a group devoted to the promotion of Software Development in the Hunter. The group meets regularly on the 1st Wednesday of each month in ICT building at the University of Newcastle. We are not limited to any particular discipline or platform and welcome anyone with an interest in coding.

Share

The meetings usually feature a guest speaker talking about a recent advance in the world of software development. There is usually a chance for technical questions to be answered and to socialise with your peers in the Hunter. It's a great opportunity to learn new skills and stay in touch with what's happening in the region and around the world.

Collaborate

And the best part - this valuable resource is all free. It's an enjoyable evening for anyone who has an interest in learning about the latest technology as well as new approaches and techniques to designing and developing software.
